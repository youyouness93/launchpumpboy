About PumpBoy

PumpBoy is a retro-futuristic tool designed to transform how users explore, track, and analyze new tokens created on Pump.fun. Combining nostalgic design with cutting-edge blockchain technology, PumpBoy offers a unique, fun, and intuitive way to discover the next big opportunities in crypto.
